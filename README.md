❤️ Heart Attack Prediction & Risk Analysis


Tools: Python, SQL, Tableau

Objective:
Identify high-risk patients and predict the likelihood of heart attacks using clinical and demographic data.

Process:
Cleaned and analysed 4,000+ patient records.
Identified major medical and demographic risk factors.
Built a logistic regression model achieving 84% accuracy.
Developed interactive Tableau dashboards highlighting key predictors and risk distributions.

Impact:
Enabled clinicians and stakeholders to clearly understand risk drivers, supporting better preventive strategies and patient prioritization.
